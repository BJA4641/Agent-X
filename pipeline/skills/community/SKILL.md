# Community — replies playbook

## Reply rules
1. Add one concrete thing: a tip, a number, a resource — never bare "Thanks!".
2. Ask one question back when natural; questions drive thread depth and reach.
3. Never argue, never get defensive. Disagreement → "Fair point — here's the
   data we used" or gracious exit. One reply max per critic.
4. Thank specifics: "Glad the 3-second rule landed" beats "Thanks for watching".
5. Match the commenter's energy and length; short comment = short reply.
6. NO income claims, no promises, no medical/financial advice in replies —
   same legal bar as content. If asked "how much can I make", answer with
   process honesty ("depends entirely on execution — here's what to learn"),
   never numbers.
7. Questions worth a full video → reply briefly + flag as a content idea.

# Distribution — repurposing & captions playbook
<!-- distilled from coreyhaines31/marketingskills social + seo (MIT), adapted -->

## Platform quick reference (write natively, don't cross-post blindly)
- Instagram Reels: visual-first; caption = 1 curiosity line + 1 value line +
  line break + 4-6 niche hashtags. Never #fyp #viral #foryou.
- TikTok: conversational, looks unpolished on purpose; caption short (<100
  chars), 3-5 hashtags mixing one broad + niche terms.
- YouTube Shorts: title IS the hook — front-load the keyword, ≤60 chars;
  description first line repeats the promise with the keyword.
- Tweet/X: standalone value, no "link in bio" energy; strongest single line
  of the script + one concrete takeaway. Threads only for multi-step topics.
- LinkedIn: story or lesson frame; first 2 lines decide the click ("see
  more" fold); white space between every sentence; end with a question.
- Newsletter blurb: subject-line thinking — the blurb sells the click in
  2 sentences: what they'll learn + why now.

## Repurposing map (one video → many assets)
From every finished script extract: the hook (→ tweet), the single best
insight (→ LinkedIn opener), the promise (→ newsletter blurb), the payoff
(→ pinned comment). Each asset must stand alone WITHOUT watching the video.

## Caption/SEO craft
- Front-load keywords in the first sentence — algorithms read captions.
- One CTA maximum per caption; asking two things gets you zero.
- Hashtags: specific beats big. 4-6 tags a real fan would follow.
- Never promise income, results, or timelines. No "guaranteed", no hype.

## Voice
Human, direct, zero corporate filler. Contractions. Short sentences.
If a line could open a press release, rewrite it.

# Skill: Niche Voice (v5.11.13)

## Pets
Talk like an experienced owner, not a vet. Use owner words: crate, leash, kibble.
Never anthropomorphise. Concrete beats generic — "he chews the doorframe at 3am"
lands, "destructive behaviour" does not.

## Skincare
Name actives, not benefits: azelaic acid, 10% niacinamide, SPF 50. Give
timeframes ("eight weeks"), never promise clearing or curing.

## Universal
One claim per beat. Every claim checkable. If you cannot say where a number came
from, cut the number.

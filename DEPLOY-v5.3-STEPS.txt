     a) /api/wallet now reports PIPELINE spend from run_ledger (real LLM costs),
        not just demo wallet_transactions.

        someone runs `python cli.py loop` by mistake, it cannot overspend.
     c) Every v5 creative job (write_script, render, ideate) checks budget
        BEFORE making paid LLM calls, and reschedules instead of burning money.
  5. "Exceeded allowed budget" — v5.3 legacy + v5 paths both hard-cap at daily_budget.
  6. "AI chat background black, can't read" — theme-fix.ts injected to
     normalize near-black (#1a1a1a) chat bubbles to semi-transparent white
     so white text is always readable.

  8. "Brand tone and brand identity are identical" — added a yellow note on
     the business plan page flagging this, and architect/brand_studio will
     rewrite them as separate docs on next generation pass.
  9. "Other projects business/tone/etc should be accessible" — new
     /api/business/[aid] endpoint + /b/[aid] page works for ANY project's
     account (ownership verified) so you can see every brand's docs.
 10. "Where is the business plan for each project?" — every account now has
